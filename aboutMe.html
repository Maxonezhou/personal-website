This is My Story

I'm Calgary born and raised, wander from coast to coast, and an avid fan of country music.

As a child, creativity played a large role in my upbringing. Whether it was drawing or music, I found joy in being able to express myself through these different mediums. Today, I am an electrical engineering student with a passion for hardware, yet, the creative roots instilled upon me still lay deep within my work. My passion for hardware and design have given me the goal to combine these diverse fields, bringing together the technical elements of hardware engineering with the creative nature of design. With this, I hope to create innovative products built from the ground up, creating memorable user experiences with exceptional technical expertise.
